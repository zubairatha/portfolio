## 5. HNSW: the shortcut graph

## 6. RRF fusion: why not both?

* each system gives ranks.

* a doc at rank 1 gives ~1/61 points.
* a doc at rank 10 gives ~1/70 points.
* if a doc is high in *either* BM25 or dense, it bubbles up.
